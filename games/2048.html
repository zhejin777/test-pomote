Game Description

2048 is an addictive puzzle game where you merge identical tiles to create higher-valued tiles. The goal is to reach the 2048 tile. Use your arrow keys to move all tiles in one direction. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one with their combined value.

Controls

  • or W Move Up
  • or S Move Down
  • or A Move Left
  • or D Move Right

Tips & Strategies

  • Keep your highest-value tile in a corner
  • Build a chain of decreasing values
  • Focus on one direction for movement
  • Plan a few moves ahead
  • Avoid using the "bad" direction unless necessary
