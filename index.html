About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and see how long you can survive against the relentless hordes.

The game features:

  • Multiple playable characters with unique abilities
  • Dozens of upgrades and power-ups to collect
  • Various enemy types with different attack patterns
  • Procedurally generated levels for endless replayability
  • Challenging boss fights that will test your skills

Perfect for quick gaming sessions or extended play, Monster Survivors offers a challenging experience that will keep you coming back for more!

Game Guide

Getting Started

When you first start Monster Survivors, you'll need to choose a character. Each character has different starting stats and a unique special ability. As you play and earn coins, you can unlock more characters.

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Survival Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize health and damage upgrades early
  • Collect experience gems to level up quickly
  • Don't ignore defensive upgrades like armor and speed
  • Save your special ability for emergency situations

Power-ups & Items

Throughout each run, you'll collect various power-ups that enhance your character:

  • Attack Boost - Increases damage output
  • Speed Up - Increases movement speed
  • Health Potion - Restores health points
  • Shield - Provides temporary invincibility
  • Area Attack - Damages all nearby enemies

Enemy Types

You'll encounter various monster types, each with different behaviors:

  • Slimes - Slow but can appear in large numbers
  • Wolves - Fast and attack in packs
  • Golems - Slow but very tough and deal high damage
  • Wizards - Attack from a distance with magic projectiles
  • Bosses - Unique enemies with special attack patterns
